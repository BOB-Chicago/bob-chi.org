Bitcoin & Open Blockchain Chicago

We are one of Chicago’s largest crypto interest groups. We get very excited by permissionless innovation and uncensorable money. We see that the social implications are deep. We can’t seem to stop thinking about the economics of the new system. We gather over drinks to talk about exciting CS that promises better blockchains. No matter what you like about Bitcoin and other “crypto” projects, you’ll find plenty of good company here.

Events

Our events are on meetup.com

We offer three kinds of events:

  • {social} Come have a beer and some pizza with us! Our May social was on the news.
  • {talk} Listen to an expert speak about an interesting topic.
  • {workshop} Join us for a cryptocurrency technology case study.
